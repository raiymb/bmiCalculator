BMI Calculator

Welcome to our BMI calculator! Body Mass Index (BMI) is a widely used indicator of body fat and overall health. It is a measure of your weight in relation to your height. By calculating your BMI, you can get an idea of whether your weight is in a healthy range.

How to Use:

  1. Enter Your Weight: Input your weight in kilograms or pounds, depending on your preference.
  2. Enter Your Height: Input your height in centimeters or inches.
  3. Calculate Your BMI: Click the "Calculate" button to determine your BMI.

Interpreting Your BMI:

  • Underweight: BMI less than 18.5
  • Normal Weight: BMI between 18.5 and 24.9
  • Overweight: BMI between 25 and 29.9
  • Obese: BMI of 30 or greater

Note: BMI is a general screening tool and does not consider factors such as muscle mass or distribution of fat. Consult with a healthcare professional for a more accurate assessment of your health.

Remember, a healthy lifestyle involves a balanced diet and regular physical activity. Use this tool as a starting point for your health journey.
